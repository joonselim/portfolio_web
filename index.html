Project · 02 / 04
Product planning

Audible Read & Listen — scroll-to-seek prototype

Live demo audiblereadandlisten…vercel.app/player ↗

Product thesis

Audiobook text should not be chained to the playhead. Readers want to browse ahead, jump back, and read-without-listening — the current UX doesn't let them.

Interaction design

  • Locked (default) — text auto-scrolls with audio
  • First scroll surfaces a "scroll up to unlock" hint
  • Unlocked — text freezes, every word becomes tappable
  • Tap a word → playback seeks there, text re-locks
  • "Go back to 1:49" pill remembers the pre-seek position

Precedents studied

YouTube Music · lyrics Spotify · lyrics Audible mobile app

Why

Music apps solved scroll-to-seek on lyrics years ago. Long-form reading deserves the same freedom — and the fix is interaction design, not a new model or backend.
